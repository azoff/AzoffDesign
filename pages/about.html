Azoff Design

What is Azoff Design?

Azoff Design is the brain-child and design portfolio of Jonathan Azoff (Me!). Starting as an over-architected web-application, it has since morphed into a Wordpress Blog, and is now living now as a project-driven, App-Engine portfolio. Mostly for selfish reasons, I started this site as a repository for my code and projects. However, the experience of sharing code online has had unexpected influences on my reasons for continuing development on this site. Its almost like a feel-good, charitable hobby that gives back every time I produce a piece of code. In the future, I hope for this site to continue living as a repository for my code, but with the concentrated aim of giving back to developers like myself. Heck, even this site is even open sourced!.

Who is Jonathan Azoff?

I am a web-developer, graphic designer, software architect and human being living in the city of San Francisco. I attended college at the University of Santa Clara, and graduated in the summer of 2009 with a Bachelors of Science in Computer Engineering. Outside of programming, I tend to read a lot, blog, and enjoy life with my friends and family. I'm no athlete, but I also spend time playing organized sports with my friends and co-workers, and have recently discovered an affinity for ping pong. As I continue my career, I hope to grow both as a professional developer and as a contributor to open-source technology.

Do you do freelance work?

I wish I could categorically say 'no', but I am too much of a dreamer to deny that the right project might not coerce my imagination. However, when taking freelance into account, I always consider my current employment status and existing non-compete agreements before making any decisions.
